This game is a return to basics

Remember when there were a lot of Flash games? This game is made in P5.js which means it can still work. Perhaps this is the future of "crappy" online games we played as a kid.

Genre

This is a very simple sidescroller. Cruise through the city on your pirate ship dodging the cars.

Keep in mind that the programmer for this game is not a proffesional. He is just a young man with 5 months of experience ...which means that this game is a technical marvel

Controls

  • Use the arrow keys to dodge up and down
